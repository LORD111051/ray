Why Salicylic Acid is Beneficial for Skin and Hair

Salicylic acid is a beta hydroxy acid that penetrates deep into pores, unclogging them and preventing acne. For hair, it helps remove dandruff and excess oil, promoting a healthy scalp.

Benefits of Lip Gloss

Lip gloss not only adds shine but also hydrates and protects lips from dryness, making them look fuller and more youthful.

Why Toner with Acids is Useful for Acne-Prone Skin

Facial toners with acids like glycolic or lactic acid help exfoliate dead skin cells, reduce inflammation, and minimize the appearance of acne scars.
